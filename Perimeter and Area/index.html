Perimeter and Area

Let's learn about Perimeter and Area.



The basics of Perimeter

1. Perimeter is the number that we get by adding the length of all the sides of a figure

2. To measure the perimeter of a square we multiply a side's length by 4. Example: If the length of a side of a square is 4 cm, then the perimeter if 4 X 4, which is 16 cm

3. To measure the perimeter of a rectangle , we do length + breadth X 2. Example: If the lenght of a rectangle is 10 cm and the breadth is 5 cm, then 10 + 5 x 2.The answer is 30cm




The basics of Area

1.Area is the space occupied by an object

2.It is measured in square meters

3.To measure the area of a square, we multiply side X side

4.Example, a square with a side length of 6 cm will have an area of 36 square centimeters

5.To measure the area of a rectangle, we multiply the length by the breadth

6.Example, a rectangle with a length of 10 cm and a breadth of 5 cm with have an area of 15 square centimeters
